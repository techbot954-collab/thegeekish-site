# Geekish Free Traffic Promotion Kit

Goal: drive real, non-spam traffic to https://thegeekish.com/ using free channels.

## Do first

- Add/verify Google Search Console property for `https://thegeekish.com/`.
- Submit `https://thegeekish.com/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console.
- Add/verify Bing Webmaster Tools and submit the same sitemap.
- Keep `robots.txt` live at `https://thegeekish.com/robots.txt`.
- Post every new article as: website post -> Instagram Reel/carousel -> TikTok/Short -> community discussion.

## Free channels to use

### Social profiles

Update bio/link fields anywhere Geekish has accounts:

> Geekish = tech news without boring tech-site energy. AI, gadgets, apps, gaming tech, creator tools, and internet culture. Read/watch: https://thegeekish.com/

### Launch / directory style posts

Short version:

> I’m building Geekish — a tech news/blog site for AI, gadgets, apps, gaming tech, creator tools, and internet culture, written to feel more visual and social-first than traditional tech sites. Feedback welcome: https://thegeekish.com/

> I’m building Geekish, a technology news and blog site focused on quick, clean, social-first tech coverage: AI, gadgets, apps, gaming tech, creator tools, and internet culture. The goal is to make tech news feel less boring and easier to turn into Reels, TikToks, Shorts, and quick reads. I’d love feedback on the direction, layout, and topics: https://thegeekish.com/

### Reddit/community-friendly angle

Do not spam the link. Use this only where self-promotion is allowed or in feedback threads:

> I’m working on a small tech news/blog project called Geekish. The idea is short, visual-first tech coverage — AI, gadgets, apps, creator tools, gaming tech — that can work as both articles and short-form clips. I’m looking for feedback more than clicks right now: https://thegeekish.com/

## Weekly free traffic rhythm

- Monday: publish/update one useful evergreen article.
- Tuesday: post one Reel/TikTok/Short pointing to the article.
- Wednesday: answer 2-3 relevant community questions without spam.
- Thursday: post a carousel or image summary.
- Friday: share “what we’re watching” tech radar.
- Weekend: check Search Console/Bing for indexing and search queries.

## Static image post workflow

Not every promotion needs video. For each article, create one bold static image first:

1. Pull the strongest headline from the article.
2. Use a neon Geekish tech background.
3. Add a short CTA: “Read the full quick take at thegeekish.com”.
4. Post as Instagram/Facebook feed image, Story, and optionally LinkedIn/X image post.
5. Turn the same concept into a carousel or video later if it performs well.
